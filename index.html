About Me

I am a versatile game developer specializing in Game design, composition, and systems programming, with extensive experience crafting immersive audio environments for various games.

I've spent much of my game development time making audio systems & engines for large, complex games. I have also led & contributed to the design of various prototypes, pitches and trailers.
